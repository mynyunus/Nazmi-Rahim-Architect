About NRA

Built on Design Discipline and Delivery Excellence

Founded in 2011 as SANMN Design Office, the practice was established on a commitment to innovation, technical rigor, and quality outcomes. In 2019, the firm evolved into Nazmi Rahim Architect (NRA) following registration with Lembaga Arkitek Malaysia.

Today, NRA continues a consistent corporate approach centered on design excellence, effective management, and long-term client satisfaction across every project phase.

  • Design Excellence & Innovation
  • Effective Project Management & Delivery
