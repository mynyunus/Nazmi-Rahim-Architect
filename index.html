About NRA

Design Discipline, Technical Clarity, and Project Delivery Focus

Founded in 2011 as SANMN Design Office, the practice later evolved into Nazmi Rahim Architect (NRA) in 2019 after registration with Lembaga Arkitek Malaysia. The firm continues to deliver architecture and interior design services anchored on innovation, quality control, and practical execution.

NRA has served residential, commercial, industrial, hospitality, and healthcare sectors in Malaysia, with a delivery model centered on coordinated teamwork between architects, technicians, and drafting teams.

Vision & Mission

Architecture should connect with people and its surroundings.

Nazmi Rahim Architect, with its team of architects and assistants is dedicated to create Architecture that strive for excellence. We are constantly creating buildings that “inspire” and at the same time connecting both the natural and man-made environment. We are committed to design green sustainable buildings to serve the environments well.

We provide a full range of architectural and interior design services. Our work emphasises practical solutions and technical innovations in order to cleverly achieve the unique design aspiration that we strive for.

We are extremely proficient at performing architectural services for various scales; from towers, large scale mixed-use complexes, exhibition facilities, industrial facilities, corporate offices, hotel to residential buildings.

Key People

Principal

Ar. Muhammad Nazmi Abdul Rahim

Bach. Sc. Architecture • Bach. Arch (Hons) • LAM A/M 281 • PAM G-3500

Ar. Nazmi Rahim graduated from Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM), Shah Alam. He worked at Arkitek Alam Sekitar Sdn. Bhd. in Kelana Jaya from 2011 to 2012 and Perunding Alam Bina Sdn. Bhd. in Kuala Lumpur from 2012 to 2019 before founding Nazmi Rahim Architect.

He is a corporate member of Lembaga Arkitek Malaysia and Pertubuhan Arkitek Malaysia. His passion for outstanding architecture continues to shape the design principles shared by the entire team.

With more than 10 years of architectural experience, he has contributed to major projects across infrastructure, healthcare, commercial, and residential sectors, including:

  • KLCC Lot K Office Tower
  • MRT Line 1 stations
  • Ipoh - Padang Besar Double Track Railway project
  • Refurbishment of Menara Millenium
  • Selangor Convention Center mixed development (Selangor Business Capital)
  • Putrajaya Endocrine Hospital
  • KTMB Commuter Depot, Seremban
  • Residential developments under Sime Darby

His delivery exposure includes work with clients such as:

  • Sime Darby Properties
  • KLCC Projeks Sdn. Bhd.
  • Gamuda
  • IJM Construction
  • George Kent Bhd
  • PKNS
  • JKR

He has also collaborated with international architectural practices, including:

  • Pelli Clarke Pelli Architects (KLCCP Office Tower)
  • COX Architecture (Selangor Convention Center)
  • Woods Bagot (Menara Millenium)
  • Foster + Partners (office tower design proposal in Kuala Lumpur)

Ar. Nazmi Rahim was invited as a guest speaker for JKR BIM Day in 2017, is a trained BIM coordinator, and has hands-on experience with GBI-certified buildings.
